About The Artist

My name is Atalya Sigler and I love helping people. I have been a graphic designer since 2020 and am looking for my next full-time or freelance opportunity. My favorite type of projects are where I can invest in people's dreams to create a dynamic design. I am always excited about any way my skills can help achieve a goal. When I am not diligently working on design projects, I enjoy playing soccer and occasionally pickleball. I can be pretty competitive and having an outlet for that is important to me. I have received the blessing of playing soccer at the collegiate level as a captain at Wake Tech Community College and learned great leadership, communication, and teamwork skills. I have also been able to travel the world, visiting 9 countries, and live in 3 different countries and two States.
